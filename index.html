How PowerSplit works

  1. Plug smart adapters into key devices or rooms in your flat.
  2. Tag each device as private (owned by one person) or shared.
  3. PowerSplit calculates everyone’s fair share and lets you settle up in one tap.

Starter Kit

4 smart plugs, a mains clamp and app access — tuned for 3–5 person flat-shares.

Upgrade later to PowerSplit Plus for usage history, exports and landlord-friendly PDFs.

Add-ons

  • Extra smart plug — extend tracking to more rooms or devices.
  • Spare labels — keep ownership crystal-clear.
  • PowerSplit Plus — deeper analytics, export tools, and optional landlord view.
