What this prototype focuses on

  • High-level explainer for students in shared flats.
  • Storefront for a physical “starter kit”.
  • Account creation and login views.
  • Simple dashboard to visualise a fair bill split.
The final website adds more polish, animations and a cookie banner for compliance.

Starter Kit

Includes smart plugs, a mains clamp and access to the PowerSplit app. Designed for 3–5 person flat-shares.

In this prototype, buttons do not take payment; they show the basic purchase journey.

Add-ons

  • Extra smart plug for more rooms/devices.
  • Replacement labels to keep ownership clear.
  • Optional analytics subscription in the final version.

Why this layout (prototype)

The storefront here is deliberately simple: one main product and a few add-ons, so the tester can focus on understanding the concept rather than complex pricing.

Create account

Create a household profile and invite your housemates.

In the final site, this flow is expanded with clearer error states and privacy copy.

Login

For this prototype, login is a simple text field that loads a sample dashboard.

This keeps the focus on the idea rather than real authentication.
